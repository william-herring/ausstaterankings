About

This is a site to keep track of state and territory speedcubing rankings in Australia. Authored by William Herring.

The project is open-source (see repo) and feedback/contributions are appreciated greatly.

How it works

Users sign in using their WCA account. If they have a WCA ID, their results as stored in the WCA database are copied to a local database, along with their basic details (name, avatar). After signing in, the user is able to select their state or territory of residence in their preferences (click profile picture in the top right to navigate). Some people have been added manually by site administrators to populate the rankings. People who represent a country other than Australia but reside here are added manually by site administrators (more on this in FAQ).
