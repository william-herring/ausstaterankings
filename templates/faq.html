FAQ

Why can't I see my results?

The WCA doesn't record your state/territory of residence, so you must log in to this site if you wish to see your state ranking. Some results are added by admins, but if you don't see yourself and expected to, please log in.


How do I change my state/territory?

If you have moved to a different state/territory, you can update your residence in user preferences.


I live in Australia but do not represent the country on my WCA profile. How can I be added to the rankings?

Currently you can only record your state residence if you have Australia listed as your country in the WCA. However, if you live in Australia, please send me an email and I will add you to the rankings.


Why aren't the results up to date?

To avoid querying the WCA database on every visit, results are updated every Wednesday. Generally, this will mean that results will be updated pretty soon after a competition's results are uploaded to the WCA database. If not, don't stress; they will update soon.


How can I help?
