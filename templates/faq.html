FAQ

Why can't I see my results?

The WCA doesn't record your state/territory of residence, so you must log in to this site and set your state if you wish to see your state ranking. Some results are added by admins, but if you don't see yourself and expected to, please log in.


How do I change my state/territory?

If you have moved to a different state/territory, you can update your residence in user preferences.


I live in Australia but do not represent the country on my WCA profile. How can I be added to the rankings?

Currently you can only record your state residence if you have Australia listed as your country in the WCA. However, if you live in Australia, please send me an email and I will add you to the rankings.


Why aren't the results up to date?

To avoid querying the WCA database on every visit, results are updated in bulk automatically every Wednesday. This means that results are usually updated pretty soon after recent competitions' results have been uploaded. If results haven't updated and you are sure that they should have,

email me.

How can I help?

This site is completely open-source. If you have any suggestions or improvements and are familiar with Flask and/or the WCA API, check out the GitHub repository.
